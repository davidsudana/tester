Our Product

Citronella Oil

Citronella oil is the result of distillation of leaves of the plant Cymbopogon Nardus citronella oil contain sitranella , geranioil and citronellol which are able to inhibit the activity of bacteria. This oil is also beneficial for aromatherapy and skin care oils.

  • Appreance : Liquid
  • Colour : Pale Yellow to Brown Yellow
  • Odor : Reminicent of Citronella

Vetiver Oil

Vetiver oil is the result of the distillation of root of the plant Chrysopogon zizanioides. This oil has a very strong aroma that is useful for overcoming insomnia and preventing anxiety.

  • Appreance : Liquid
  • Colour : Light brown to dark brown
  • Odor : Sweet & woody

Patchouli Oil

Patchouli oil is the result of the distillation of leaves of the plant Pogostemon Cablin. This oil is beneficial for health and aromatherapy.

  • Appreance : Liquid
  • Colour : Dark brown
  • Odor : Leafy

Nutmeg Oil

Nutmeg oil is the result of the distillation of nutmeg seeds that have beenseparated by their shells. This oil is very useful for relieving stress andreducing pain. This oil can also be used as a skin care ingredient.

  • Appreance : Liquid
  • Colour : Colourless to pale yellow / soft yellow
  • Odor : Spicy

Clove Leaf Oil

Clove leaf oil is the result of the distillation of leaves of the plant SyzygiumAromaticum. This oil contains anti-bacterial and anti-fungal properties. This oil also hasaromatherapy which is very beneficial.

  • Appreance : Liquid
  • Colour : Yellow
  • Odor : Spicy , woody

Cananga (Ylang-Ylang) Oil

Cananga oil or as ylang ylang oil is the result of the distillation of flower ofthe cananga odorate tree.

  • Appreance : Liquid
  • Colour : Soft yellow
  • Odor : Floral , sweet , slightly woody
